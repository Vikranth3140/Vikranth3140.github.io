Hello, I'm

Vikranth Udandarao

AI researcher and data scientist building reliable agentic systems for real-world decision-making.

Applied GenAi @ United Airlines · B.Tech CSAI @ IIIT Delhi · AI Research

I am a B.Tech graduate in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence from IIIT Delhi, working at the intersection of academic research and production-grade AI deployment. As a Researcher at the Multimodal Digital Media Analysis (MIDAS) Lab, my work focuses on building explainable multi-agent systems, designing complex agentic workflows, and evaluating LLM guardrails for financial reasoning and safety. My research has been published at AAAI, WWW, ECML PKDD, CODS-COMAD, and India HCI, and I was honored as a National Winner of the Securities Market Hackathon by SEBI, NSE, and BSE.

On the applied side, I engineer enterprise-scale AI pipelines. I currently work as an Associate Data Scientist at United Airlines, building reliable LLM orchestration, RAG, and multi-agent systems for enterprise aviation workflows. Previously, as an AI Engineer Intern at SayaCare, I designed and quantized low-latency OCR-LLM inference pipelines for medical document intelligence. I previously served as the President of BYLD (IIITD's software development club), where I led student developers and oversaw institute-wide portals used daily by 1,000+ students and faculty.

My mission is to move AI beyond isolated sandbox demos into safe, reliable, and highly-optimized real-world deployments.
